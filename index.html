Why Yoga?

In present era, mankind is using high level technology in day to day life to make life easier. While ‘health’ and ‘peace of mind’ has become a big worry in human society. Large number of population is living with dependency on medicines. This all is due to highly competitive environment, rapid increase in inflation, collapse of joint family culture, decline in moral values etc. has put the mankind under heavy stress. This causes decrease in immunity, Irritable nature and many other psycho-physical changes in human beings. In addition, polluted environment, genetically altered food grains & vegetables, extensive use of chemical fertilizer and pesticides in agriculture has also affected human health. Overall effect of above all finally appears as Chronic diseases, psychological disorders and/or psychosomatic diseases. This marginally affects the efficiency, capabilities and behavior of person. It has been proved by many scientific and medical researches that Yoga practice is the best way to reduce and overcome effect of above all. Yoga, not only cures physical and psychological disorders, it gives peace of mind, improves synergy in all of levels of consciousness. Regular yoga practice ensures good health, better efficiency and harmony in family and at workplace. If a group of people / organization wishes, we can offer a free session on “The Indian traditional Yoga, and its roll in elimination of various diseases.” Benefit of this session will be that you would get to know the actual Indian traditional yoga and its therapeutic benefits.


Thanking you.

Who is Mukesh Chauhan?

Basically an Engineer, worked as Project Engineer then as Management Consultant and auditor of ISO 9000 & 14000 for more than 12 years. Has more than 16 years of experience in therapeutic yoga. He teaches subjects like ‘Yog Therapy’ and ‘Patanjali Yog Sutras’ in the Master degree and PG Diploma in Yoga at Yog dept. of Gujarat Vidyapeeth, Ahmedabad. Conducts personal and group yoga classes (since last 16 years) Guided persons of all age groups for yoga practices. People suffering from various chronic diseases like Diabetes, Hypertension (high BP), abnormal level of Cholesterol, Cardiovascular diseases, Prostate related disorders, Asthma, Brain related ailments, like Parkinson’s, Migraine & other kind of headaches, Snoring, Insomnia, Depression, Hyper activity in children. All persons guided by him for yoga practice has been benefited, got relief / came out of disease, satisfied according to their expectations.
